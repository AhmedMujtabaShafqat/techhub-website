Our Story

Built by Technologists,
for Builders

TechHub was founded in 2012 with a single mission: make enterprise-grade technology accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission

Technology That
Empowers People

We believe technology should be an enabler, not a barrier. TechHub was born from the frustration of watching organisations struggle with brittle, overpriced, and poorly supported systems.

Today we serve over 5,000 clients across 40 countries — from early-stage startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our approach stays the same: listen first, build second, support always.

Our Journey

A Decade of Innovation

2012

Founded in Manchester

TechHub opens its doors with 5 engineers and a shared belief that technology consulting should be honest and fairly priced.

2015

Cloud Practice Launched

We launch our cloud infrastructure practice, becoming one of the UK's first AWS Advanced Consulting Partners.

2018

Global Expansion

Offices open in New York, Dubai, and Singapore. Client base grows to 2,000+ organisations across 25 countries.

2021

AI Division Founded

TechHub AI launches with 40 ML engineers and data scientists, shipping intelligent solutions across industries.

2025

Today

150+ engineers, 5,000+ clients, 1,200+ delivered projects. And we're just getting started.
