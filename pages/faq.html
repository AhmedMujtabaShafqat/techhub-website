📋 General

TechHub is a full-service technology company offering cloud infrastructure, AI and machine learning, cybersecurity, web and mobile development, and data analytics services. We partner with businesses of all sizes — from startups to Fortune 500 companies — to design, build, and support the technology that powers their operations.
TechHub was founded in 2012 in Manchester, UK. Over the past 13 years we've grown from a 5-person startup to a global team of 150+ engineers serving 5,000+ clients across 40 countries.
We work with businesses of all sizes. Our Starter plan is designed specifically for small businesses and startups, while our Growth and Enterprise plans scale to serve large corporations with complex needs. We believe great technology should be accessible to everyone.
We have deep expertise across financial services, healthcare, retail and e-commerce, manufacturing, media, logistics, and the public sector. Our technology agnostic approach means we can adapt our methodology to virtually any industry vertical.

💳 Billing

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), BACS bank transfers, and SEPA transfers for European clients. All online payments are processed securely via Stripe. Enterprise clients can also be invoiced on Net 30 terms.

🔒 Security

Yes, TechHub is ISO 27001:2022 certified. This certification covers our information security management systems for all service delivery and product operations. We also undergo annual third-party penetration tests and publish a SOC 2 Type II report for Enterprise clients.
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We maintain geographic data residency controls so your data can remain in your chosen region (EU, US, APAC). We never sell or share client data with third parties.
Yes, full GDPR compliance is maintained across all our products and services. We act as a Data Processor under GDPR and provide a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) to all clients. You retain full ownership and control of your data at all times.
